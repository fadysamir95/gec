About Global Engineering Contracting

Our Story

Global Engineering Contracting (GEC) is a specialized engineering and contracting company delivering integrated solutions for industrial, commercial, and healthcare projects. We execute complete systems from design to commissioning, with a strong focus on quality, safety, and on-time delivery.

  • Integrated solutions under one contractor (MEP & Low Current)
  • Compliance with standards, testing, and commissioning documentation
  • Fast response support and preventive maintenance programs

Our team provides reliable implementation for electrical power, fire alarm, security, networks, and mechanical systems—supported by professional documentation and after-sales support.
